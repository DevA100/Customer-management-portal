# Customer Management Portal (CRM)

## Overview

The **Customer Management Portal** is an enterprise CRM web application built to manage and track **customer incident requests, support tickets, and operational issues**.

The system allows internal teams to log, monitor, update, and resolve customer incidents efficiently while maintaining clear operational visibility.

The application is designed for organizations that require structured **incident tracking, customer issue management, and service monitoring**.

---

## Key Features

### Customer Incident Management
- Log and manage customer incident requests
- Track issue status and resolution progress
- Maintain incident history for each customer

### CRM Portal
- Centralized customer information management
- Organized record of customer interactions and support requests

### Operational Tracking
- Monitor active incidents
- Track resolution timelines
- Improve response time to customer issues

### Administrative Control
- Manage incident records
- Update customer information
- Track service performance

---

## Technology Stack

- **Backend:** ASP.NET MVC (.NET)
- **Database:** SQL Server
- **ORM:** Dapper / Data Access Layer
- **Frontend:** Razor Views, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Architecture:** MVC Pattern

---

## Use Case

This system is designed to support **customer service teams and operational departments** by providing a structured platform to manage customer incidents and service requests.

It improves:

- Incident tracking
- Service response time
- Customer support efficiency
- Operational transparency

---

## Deployment

The application is currently **deployed on an on-premise server within a FinTech organization's internal infrastructure**.

It is actively used as an internal **CRM and incident management portal**.

---

## Data Protection Notice

⚠️ **Confidentiality Notice**

Due to the sensitive nature of customer and operational data handled by this system, screenshots of the dashboard and internal interfaces cannot be publicly shared.

The application runs within a **secure private enterprise environment**, and sharing interface screenshots may expose confidential customer information.
